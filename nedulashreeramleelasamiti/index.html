नेदुला श्री रामलीला समिति प्रयागराज (पंजी.) आपका हार्दिक स्वागत एवं अभिनन्दन करती है |


कृपया चैनल को सब्स्क्राइब करें तथा वीडिओज़ को ज्यादा से ज्यादा शेयर करें








Our Ramleela Committee is one of the oldest Ramleela committee in Prayagraj district of Uttar Pradesh, which started in the year 1935. After few years of its inception, Ramleela was not staged for a few years due to some social reasons, but again from 1966 AD, it has been continuously staging the grand Ramleela. Thus, this time Ramleela of 2023 is going to be the 58th Ramleela of this committee. Every year Shree Ramleela is organized for 6 days starting from Quar Shukla Saptami to Quar Shukla Dwadashi and a fair is organized on the 7th day.
